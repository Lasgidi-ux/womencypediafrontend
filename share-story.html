Tips for a Great Story

Follow these guidelines to make your submission as impactful as possible.

history_edu

Start with Context

Who is the woman? When and where did she live? What era or cultural moment shaped her life?

auto_stories

Tell the Journey

Include challenges, turning points, and achievements. The richness of detail matters.

psychology

Share the Meaning

What can others learn from this story? Why does it matter for future generations?
