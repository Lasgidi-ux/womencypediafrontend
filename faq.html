info General

Womencypedia is the world's first interpretive encyclopedia dedicated to women's stories. We document and preserve the lives, achievements, and cultural significance of women throughout history who have been overlooked or underrepresented in traditional knowledge archives. Our platform combines rigorous research with interpretive storytelling to reveal the depth, power, and cultural meaning behind every woman's story.
Yes! Womencypedia's core library of biographies, educational resources, and research materials is completely free to access. We believe knowledge about women's contributions should be accessible to everyone. We offer premium subscriptions for institutions and researchers who need advanced features like API access, bulk downloads, and specialized research tools.
While Wikipedia is a general encyclopedia, Womencypedia is specifically focused on women's stories with an interpretive approach. We provide deeper context, cultural analysis, and educational resources around each biography. Our content is verified by an editorial team, and we actively work to include women who may not meet traditional notability standards but have made significant contributions to their communities and fields.

edit_note Content & Contributions

You can nominate any woman whose story you believe should be documented through our Nomination Form. Provide as much information as possible, including her name, achievements, and any supporting references. Our editorial team will review all nominations and verify the information before publication.
Absolutely! We encourage personal and family stories through our Share Your Story feature. These stories help preserve intergenerational knowledge and celebrate the everyday heroines in our communities. All submissions go through a verification process to ensure accuracy and respect for the subjects.
Every entry goes through our rigorous verification process: (1) Initial review for completeness, (2) Cross-checking with 2-3 authoritative sources, (3) Editorial review for neutrality and accuracy, (4) Final approval and publication. We document all sources and update entries as new information becomes available. See our Methodology page for details.

account_circle Account & Privacy

No account is needed to browse and read our content. However, creating a free account allows you to save bookmarks, track reading history, submit nominations and stories, and receive personalized recommendations. Premium accounts offer additional features for researchers and institutions.
We take privacy seriously. Your personal information is never sold to third parties. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, and you can delete your account at any time. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details on how we collect, use, and protect your information.
Yes, you can delete your account at any time through your Account Settings. Account deletion will remove your personal information and saved data. Note that any contributions you've made (nominations, stories) will remain as part of Womencypedia's archive but will be anonymized.

school Education & Research

Yes! Educators are welcome to use our content for non-commercial educational purposes. We provide ready-made lesson plans and educational modules in our Education section. For institutional licensing or commercial use, please contact us.
Each biography page includes a citation generator that supports major citation styles (APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard). Click the "Cite" button on any page to generate the appropriate citation format. For general citations, use: "Article Title." Womencypedia, Publication Date, URL. Accessed Date.

settings Technical

Yes, we offer a REST API for researchers and developers. The API provides access to our biography database, search functionality, and educational resources. API access is included with institutional subscriptions. Contact us for API documentation and access credentials.
Yes! Womencypedia is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your device for offline access to previously viewed content. On mobile devices, use the "Add to Home Screen" option in your browser. On desktop, look for the install icon in your browser's address bar.
