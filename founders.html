Dedication

For every woman whose story was ever overlooked, minimized, or forgotten.

For the women whose names were never written,
Whose contributions were never recorded,
Whose brilliance was never acknowledged,
And whose legacies were scattered across time.

For the women who led quietly and those who led boldly.
For the women who shaped communities, cultures, movements, and nations.

For Mary Mitchell Slessor and Rev. Mgbeke George Okore,
Whose stories illuminated the path that led to this institution.

For The University of Edinburgh,
Where this vision was sharpened, nurtured, and transformed into purpose.

And for every woman still waiting to be found —
We are coming for you.
