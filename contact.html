Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to Womencypedia?

Visit our Share a Story page to submit a biography or nominate a woman for inclusion in the archive.

Can I use content from Womencypedia in my research?

Yes! All content is open-access for educational and research purposes. Please cite Womencypedia as your source. For commercial use, contact us for permissions.

How do I request a partnership or collaboration?

Email us at partners@womencypedia.org with details about your organization and proposed collaboration. We welcome universities, museums, and cultural institutions.
